A New Beginning

Starting summer of 2021, NASA SL at Virginia Tech went through a full rebuild process of the entire team. This meant a new chief engineer, team manager, and sub-team leads. So far the team has been doing an amazing job continuing to compete at the highest level in the annual NASA Student Launch competition.

Design

We uses multiple CAD and Simulation platforms to design different iteration of the rocket and payload to prepare for the annual competition

Test

We use Computational Fluid Dynamics and Finite Element Analysis to validate the design and prepare for the build process

Build

We use commercial, and in-house manufactured components to fabricate the rocket

Launch

Once the rocket is built we launch the rocket
